Food Logging and Tracking:
Comprehensive Food Database: Include a vast database of foods, including brand-name products and restaurant meals, with accurate nutritional information. 
Barcode Scanner: Enable users to scan barcodes on packaged foods to quickly add items to their logs. 
Manual Food Entry: Allow users to manually enter food items and their quantities. 
Meal Logging: Enable users to group food items into meals (e.g., breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks). 
Nutrient Tracking: Track calories, macronutrients (protein, carbohydrates, fats), and other key nutrients (vitamins, minerals). 
Water Tracking: Allow users to log their water intake. 
Customizable Units: Allow users to choose their preferred units of measurement (e.g., grams, ounces, cups).
